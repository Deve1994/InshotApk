How to Remove Watermarks from InShot App

Without wasting a single moment, we are now delving deep down to follow steps to remove the watermark from the InShot app. Follow these steps to get the watermark removed for your editing.

For Android Users

1. Open Google Play Store on your phone and type "InShot Pro".
2. Click on the app with the Pro version.
3. Click on purchase/buy or upgrade version to get it.

For iOS Users

1. Open the App Store on your phone and type "InShot Pro".

Once your purchase is done, your InShot Pro app will be installed on your device. Now, you are ready to use its pro features like the removal of watermarks.

Step 3: Import Your Video or Photo

Step 4: Edit Your Photos and Videos as Per Your Demand

In InShot premium version, you can add any features as per your video or photo demand to make them more stunning without fear of watermarks issue.

Step 5: Export Without Watermark

Once you are done with video or photo editing, export them and then just click on the Save option on top of the screen to save them.
